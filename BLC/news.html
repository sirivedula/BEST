IN THE NEWS

BLC provides small group and one-on-one tutoring for students K-12 in all subjects. We are looking for highly talented tutors who can relate to the students and enthusiastically motivate them to succeed. Tutors need a strong grasp of the subject matter, and must enjoy interacting with people and lastly; they must be open to feedback. We are a staff of nearly 20, making us one of the largest employers of UC Merced students and Alumni.
